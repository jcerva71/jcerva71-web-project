So what is Midwest Emo?

Midwest Emo is a subgenre that originated during the 1990's often categorized as "second-wave emo" or "post-emo indie rock."

Some common characteristics include:

  • "twinkly" guitar tones
  • heartfelt, self-deprecating lyrics
  • Intricate melodies
  • Math rock sounds

Although its origins come from the 90's, there has been a revival in current times
